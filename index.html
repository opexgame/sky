Roadmap

Q1 — Waitlist & Codes (January 2026)

• Code distribution: each code equals a fixed number of tokens and one Skypuffs NFT waitlist slot.
• 1 code = 1 waitlist slot.
• Tasks to validate the waitlist:
• Play the Discord mini-game and earn 77 coins.
• Submit your wallet.
• Post a tweet in the requested format.
• Pass verification (auto or manual).
✅ Valid waitlist = confirmed slot.

Q2 — Fire Sale

• Guaranteed Allowlist → early validated users get secured access.
• Non-guaranteed Waitlist → raffle participation with early-entry boosts:
• Standard entry = 1 ticket entry < 1h = 2 tickets (exact boost to be defined).
• Fixed price (USD & SKY to be defined).
• Results & refunds announced within 48h after the Fire Sale ends.

Q3 — Unlocking (Fire Sale & Private Sale)

• Fire Sale: 25% unlocked instantly; 75% released linearly each day over 12 months.
• Private Sale: 15% until month 6 ; 10% unlocked at month 6; remaining 90% released linearly each day over 18 months (total 24 months).
• Claim window: 69 days after each unlock; otherwise returned to the Ecosystem Fund.

Q4 — Airdrops

• For SKYPUFFS NFT holders: large airdrops based on type and traits.
• For Maps & Valuables: burn the NFT → convert to SKY tokens (amount depends on rarity).
• Unlocking: part unlocked instantly; remainder vested linearly over 24 months.

Q5 — Token Launch

• SKY officially live.
• Claim and refunds activated.
• Key deadlines (TBD): Maps/Valuables claims, Fire Sale full unlock, Private Sale full unlock.

Q6 — Ecosystem Fund (Post-unlock)

• Any SKY unclaimed within 77 days automatically returns to the Ecosystem Fund.
• Purpose: reuse tokens for project growth (new partnerships, incentives, community rewards, liquidity).
